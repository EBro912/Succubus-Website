Succubus Privacy Policy

Succubus takes your privacy and data seriously. Below is everything you need to know about your privacy and personal data when using Succubus.

We will never sell or share your information for advertising or marketing means, nor share your data with any third party, except when it is required by law.

All of the information listed below is collected fairly and lawfully with your full knowledge and consent.

Succubus saves the following Discord related information in a Virginia located secure server in order to function:

- Message IDs when a message is added to any servers Starboard.
- Role IDs and Emote IDs for Reaction Roles.
- Guild IDs and Channel IDs for various configuration options including Logging, Welcome messages, and Moderation.
- User IDs for some moderation features and when joining a Ping Club.
- User provided content including but not limited to welcome images and messages, ping clubs and their names, autoban configurations, and Hololive alerts.

Any other user related data is not collected by Succubus, and is instead retrieved lawfully using the Discord API.

The above information is only retained for as long as it is needed, and the data that is collected is protected to prevent loss, theft, modification, copying, and unauthorized access.



If you have any questions or concerns about the information listed above, you may join the Support Server for more information or to request to opt out. However, you will lose much functionality of the bot in doing so.
